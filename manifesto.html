The NOTHING Manifesto

0. The Void is Everything

In the digital realm, nothing is as powerful as Nothing itself. We embrace the absence, the void, the null state as the ultimate form of expression.

1. Digital Entropy

All digital artifacts tend toward decay. We accelerate this natural process, finding beauty in the dissolution of data.

2. Anti-Content

We reject the infinite scroll. We embrace the empty viewport. The absence of content is our content.

3. NULL = TRUE

In a world of infinite information, only nothing remains meaningful. We are the custodians of digital silence.
